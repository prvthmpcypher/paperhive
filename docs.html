PaperHive Legal Docs

Effective date: 16 June 2026

This page contains all legal and policy documents for the PaperHive app in one place: privacy policy, terms of service, acceptable use, security notes, disclaimer, cookie/local-storage notes, and contact information. This is not legal advice.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service Acceptable Use Security Notes Disclaimer Legal Contact

1. Privacy Policy

PaperHive is designed as a client-side, offline-first PDF toolkit. The app does not intentionally upload your PDFs, passwords, images, annotations, page ranges, redactions, signatures, or text notes to a server.

1.1 Files and passwords

Selected files are read into browser memory so they can be rendered, edited and rebuilt. PDF passwords are used only to open protected files in the current browser session. PaperHive does not intentionally store passwords in cookies, localStorage, analytics, server logs or databases.

1.2 No analytics or ads

This build does not include analytics scripts, advertising networks, remote fonts, tracking pixels or hidden API calls. Social icons are outbound links that load third-party sites only if clicked.

1.3 Offline cache

If served over HTTPS or localhost, a service worker may cache application files such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PDF.js assets, fonts and icons. This cache is for the app shell only and does not intentionally store your selected PDF files or passwords.

1.4 Local storage

The app avoids storing document content in localStorage. If you modify the app later, document persistence should be clearly disclosed and opt-in.

2. Terms of Service

By using PaperHive, you agree to use it only for lawful purposes and only with documents you own, created, control, or have clear permission to unlock, edit, convert, redact or export.

2.1 Client-side tool

PaperHive is a static browser application. You are responsible for your files, passwords, browser environment, downloaded outputs, backups and device security.

2.2 Flattened exports

Unlocked and edited PDFs are rebuilt from rendered page images. This generally removes the open password from the new copy and flattens visible edits, but it may not preserve selectable text, form fields, comments, bookmarks, layers, metadata, embedded files, scripts, accessibility tags or digital signatures.

2.3 Changes

Features and docs may change. For production use, publish versioned docs and review them with a qualified legal professional.

3. Acceptable Use and Legal Use

  • Do not use PaperHive to bypass access controls on documents you are not authorized to process.
  • Do not remove passwords from documents that belong to another person or organization without permission.
  • Do not use the tool to hide fraud, forge documents, violate privacy, infringe copyright, or misrepresent records.
  • Always verify redactions and exported documents before sharing them externally.

4. Security Notes

PaperHive uses a privacy-first design, but browser-based PDF processing has practical limitations.

Passwords

Use the clear-session button after processing sensitive files, especially on shared devices.

Redaction

Redaction boxes are flattened into the exported PDF. Do not distribute the original source file if it contains sensitive information.

Large files

Very large PDFs can use significant RAM and may fail depending on the browser or device.

Offline mode

For best offline behavior, serve the app from HTTPS or localhost so the service worker can cache assets.

5. Disclaimer and Limitation of Liability

PaperHive is provided “as is” without warranties of any kind. The app may fail on corrupted files, unusual encryption, unsupported browsers, very large files, or documents requiring advanced PDF features. To the maximum extent allowed by law, the maker is not responsible for data loss, legal claims, unauthorized use, business interruption or damages arising from use of this app.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This app does not set tracking cookies. If you deploy PaperHive with hosting analytics, payment tools, authentication, support chat, or other third-party scripts, update this section before publishing.

7. Legal Contact

For legal or policy inquiries, reach out via the social links on the About page.

No legal advice is provided through this app.
