Important limitation

The "unlocked PDF" export is a flattened, rasterized rebuild generated from pages you are able to open with the correct password. It is excellent for viewing, sharing, printing, redaction, and password-free copies, but it may not preserve selectable text, layers, forms, bookmarks, or digital signatures.

Use PaperHive only for documents you own or are allowed to process.
